## Taking Flight Event Curriculum

This curriculum supports a large-scale aviation and aerospace community event featuring RC aircraft demonstrations, foam board glider workshops, FTC robotics scrimmages, and hands-on STEM activities for students and families.

### Curriculum Components
- Teacher guides, build instructions, and safety checklists
- Multi-week classroom sequence connecting physics of flight and engineering design
- Competition rubrics, milestone timelines, and documentation tools
- Materials lists, build constraints, testing procedures, and event logistics
- Community engagement strategies with partners and mentors

### Learning Framework
Grounded in engineering design-cycle practice, project-based learning, and iterative prototyping, with emphasis on teamwork, preflight inspections, and industry-style design reviews.
