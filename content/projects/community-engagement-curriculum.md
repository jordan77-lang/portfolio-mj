## Project Zephyr — High-Altitude Balloon Mission

Student-led stratospheric mission connecting aerospace engineering, atmospheric science, and data analysis.

### Watch the Mission

### Highlights
- Sensor payload design (temperature, pressure, humidity, IMU) and power budgeting
- APRS/GPS live tracking and coordinated chase/recovery
- FAA coordination, launch logistics, and risk management
- Post-flight map, data, and video analysis with student presentations
