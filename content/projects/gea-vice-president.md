## Overview

As Vice President of the Gilbert Education Association, I represented educators across Gilbert Public Schools—leading advocacy efforts, supporting member services, and collaborating with district leadership to improve teaching and learning conditions.

## Leadership Highlights

- Led and supported contract negotiations using interest‑based bargaining practices
- Helped shape policy and professional development priorities in partnership with district leadership
- Served as Site Representative and on the School Improvement Committee
- Designed the GEA logo and created the Association website to strengthen communication

## Impact

This work advanced educator voice, strengthened labor–management relationships, and improved student learning conditions by focusing on collaborative problem solving and clear communication.
